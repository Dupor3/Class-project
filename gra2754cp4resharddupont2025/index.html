Welcome to Park Seed

About us

Park Seed Company, established in 1868 by George Park, is one of the most prominent and long-standing seed and gardening supply companies in the United States. Based in Greenwood, South Carolina, it offers a vast selection of seeds for flowers, vegetables, herbs, fruits, and more, catering to both amateur gardeners and professional horticulturists. Over its long history, Park Seed has built a reputation for high-quality products, including hybrid seeds, unique plant varieties, and trusted gardening tools. The company is known for its innovation in developing new plant varieties and providing gardeners with detailed care instructions and educational resources. Their catalog is expansive, and they also sell items such as fertilizers, soil amendments, planters, and garden decor.
